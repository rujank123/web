Who Are We?

At Rk Travels & Tours, our aim is to provide a platform for cultural awareness and heritage preservation in the Himalayas through the promotion of responsible tourism. Our late founder, Das Shrestha, incorporated this vision into the base of how we operate. We believe that there is more to an object, or an experience, than simply the material; we believe that humanity exists and expresses itself in myriad unique ways, which has an intrinsic worth that speaks beyond words. This is art, this is culture, this is magic. At Rk Travels & Tours, we believe in preserving and sharing this magic.

In 1969, Rk Travels & Tours was one of the first travel agencies to be established in Nepal, at a time when the country had just started opening its doors to visitors. From humble beginnings, our team worked hard to bring the company to the forefront of the industry. We are proud that today our services are among the most professional, service-oriented and efficient found in Nepal today. At Rk Travels & Tours, we believe in the value of people and the value of an experience; providing good people with memories that will last a lifetime is our way of making this world a little wiser, a little kinder and a little bit smaller. As your experienced partner in Nepal, we invite you to an unforgettable journey. Let us make your dreams come true.

Mrs. Rita Shrestha, President






































Why Rk TT

Catering to your needs

We have services to suit all travelling styles and ambitions: from the simple traveller to those who are in pursuit of luxury and style, we can make it happen. We can arrange every element of your trip from the air and overland ticketing to the minutest needs of your itinerary.

Professional Service and Care

Our staff are carefully selected and trained to give you the highest level of service, and to make you feel at ease throughout your visit. Our staff are fluent in English, and our tour guides are fluent in German, French, Italian and Japanese. Safety First

At Rk Travels & Tours, we are experts in safe travel. As the official Nepal representative for Europe Assistance, one of Europe's largest insurance companies, we are entrusted with the responsibility of handling every aspect of an emergency, be it travel arrangements, medical assistance or even lifesaving helicopter evacuations for mountaineers.

Fleet of vehicles at your service

Here at Rk TT, we operate a fleet of cars, mini-vans and buses for all kinds of trips and tours. Our vehicles are safe, comfortable and reliable, and our drivers courteous, professional and experienced.
